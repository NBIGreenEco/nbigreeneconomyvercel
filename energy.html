Energy (Clean and Efficient)

Clean and Efficient Energy

Clean and efficient energy means using power sources like the sun and wind, which don't cause pollution. It also means using less energy by making our machines and buildings work better, reducing our carbon footprint while meeting our energy needs.

South Africa has abundant renewable energy resources, particularly solar and wind. Transitioning to clean energy sources reduces reliance on fossil fuels, creates jobs in the green energy sector, and contributes to global climate change mitigation efforts.

Renewable Sources

Harnessing solar, wind, hydro, and geothermal energy to generate electricity without greenhouse gas emissions or environmental degradation.

Energy Efficiency

Implementing technologies and practices that reduce energy consumption in buildings, transportation, and industrial processes without compromising performance.

Smart Grids

Developing intelligent electricity networks that optimize energy distribution, integrate renewable sources, and enable consumer participation in energy management.
