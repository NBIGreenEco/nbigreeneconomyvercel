Sustainable Transport & Infrastructure

Green Mobility Solutions

Sustainable transport means using greener ways to get around, such as electric cars and public transport. Sustainable infrastructure means constructing roads and buildings that are kinder to the environment, reducing carbon emissions while improving mobility.

South Africa faces significant transport challenges including congestion, pollution, and inadequate public transport. Investing in sustainable transport infrastructure creates jobs, reduces environmental impact, and improves quality of life for all citizens.

Public Transport Systems

Developing efficient, affordable, and accessible public transportation networks including buses, trains, and rapid transit systems to reduce private vehicle usage.

Electric Mobility

Promoting electric vehicles, charging infrastructure, and clean energy solutions for transportation to reduce air pollution and greenhouse gas emissions.

Smart Infrastructure

Implementing intelligent transport systems, sustainable urban planning, and green building standards to create efficient, resilient infrastructure networks.
